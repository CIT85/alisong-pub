bullet points

scrollbar

i changed the color of the scrollbar on the select for my form using: select {scrollbar-color: #fff #000;} - needs two values, one for background and one for scroller

highlight

i changed the color of the highlight on the page (when you click and drag to copy) by adding: "element"::selection {background: color;} (ex: p::selection {background: gold;}) - makes the page more cohesive in color
