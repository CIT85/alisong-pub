pottery classes in fresno

clay mix studios in fresno, ca

clay mix is a pottery studio near tower district in fresno, california. they not only sell studio supplies like clay, glazes, and tools, but they offer classes for students of all experience levels. They have local artists ome by the shop to share their expertise with students. they also offer studio firing, so any piece that you make during the class (or at home) can be fired in clay mix's kilns. there's no need to go buy expensive equipment for your hobby when clay mix is right here in the heart of town!
